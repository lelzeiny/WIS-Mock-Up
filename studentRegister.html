WIS

General Information

During the school day the students are provided with snacks which are included in the tuition. WIS students attend classes on either Saturday OR Sunday.
Saturday classes begin at 10am and end at 1pm.
Sunday classes begin at 9am and end at 12pm.
The class day is assigned to the student based on parent preference and availability. WIS holds student enrollment once a year and does not enroll students outside of that period. Please note that the WIS runs at full capacity with a limited number of available seats. The current tuition for enrollment during the entire school year is as follows:
1 Child-$320
2 Children-$600
3 Children-$860
4 or more Children-$1000
Financial Aid is available for those who apply. No one will be turned away for lack of funds.

Registration Requirements

Priority registration for returning students for the 2017-2018 school year will begin on March 25th, 2017 and end on April 30th, 2017. New student registration for the 2017-2018 school year will begin on May 6th, 2017 and end on June 4th, 2017.
